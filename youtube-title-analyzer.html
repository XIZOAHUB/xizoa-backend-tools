Free YouTube Title Analyzer & Score Checker

A great video can fail simply because the title is boring. The YouTube algorithm relies heavily on CTR (Click-Through Rate). If people see your title and don't click, YouTube stops promoting your video. Xizoa Hub's Title Analyzer uses proven psychological triggers to score your headline and tell you exactly how to fix it.

What Makes a Viral Title?

We analyze your title based on 4 key factors that top creators use:

  1. Optimal Length: Titles should be between 40-60 characters. Too short gives no info; too long gets cut off on mobile screens.
  2. Power Words: Words like "Secret", "Shocking", "Insane", "Finally", "Free" trigger emotions and curiosity.
  3. Numbers: The human brain loves lists and data. "5 Ways to..." performs better than "Ways to...".
  4. Brackets: Adding context at the end like (Official), (2025), or (Must Watch) increases clicks by up to 30%.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does title length really matter?

Yes. If your title is too long (over 70 characters), YouTube adds "..." at the end, hiding your keywords. Keep the most important words at the beginning.

Should I use ALL CAPS?

Using ALL CAPS for 1-2 words is great for emphasis (e.g., "This is HUGE"). But writing the whole title in caps looks like spam and can hurt your CTR.
