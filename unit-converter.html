The Temperature Confusion (C vs F)

Temperature conversion is unique because it doesn't start at zero.
Celsius (°C): Used globally. Water freezes at 0° and boils at 100°.
Fahrenheit (°F): Used in the US. Water freezes at 32° and boils at 212°.
Kelvin (K): Used in science. 0K is absolute zero (-273.15°C).

Digital Storage: GB vs GiB

Have you noticed that a "500GB" hard drive only shows "465GB" on your computer? This is because manufacturers use the decimal system (1GB = 1000MB), while computers use the binary system (1GB = 1024MB). Our tool allows you to convert accurately between these formats.
