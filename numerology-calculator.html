What is Numerology? Understanding Mulank & Bhagyank

Numerology is the ancient science of numbers and their influence on human life. In Vedic Numerology (Ank Jyotish), your life is primarily governed by two major numbers: Mulank and Bhagyank.

1. What is Mulank (Psychic/Birth Number)?

Mulank is calculated from the date of the month you were born. For example, if you were born on the 15th, your Mulank is 1+5 = 6. This number represents your inner self, personality, and immediate behavior. It is the number of the "Self".

2. What is Bhagyank (Life Path/Destiny Number)?

Bhagyank is the sum total of your entire date of birth (Date + Month + Year). It represents your destiny, life purpose, and the challenges you will face in the long run. While Mulank is about "who you are", Bhagyank is about "what you will become".

3. The Role of Zodiac (Rashi)

Your Zodiac sign adds another layer to your personality. While numbers govern your vibration, your Rashi governs your elemental nature (Fire, Earth, Air, Water). A combination of a strong Mulank and a compatible Rashi creates a powerful personality.
