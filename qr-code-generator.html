Free Custom QR Code Generator

In a contactless world, Quick Response (QR) codes are essential. Whether you want to share your website, allow guests to join your WiFi without typing passwords, or share your contact details, Xizoa Hub's QR Code Generator makes it easy, fast, and free.

How to Create a WiFi QR Code?

Tired of telling everyone your long WiFi password? Use our WiFi tool:

  1. Select the WiFi tab above.
  2. Enter your Network Name (SSID) exactly as it appears on your phone.
  3. Enter your WiFi Password.
  4. Click Generate. Now, anyone who scans this code will automatically connect to your internet!

Customize Your Brand

Black and white is boring. Our tool allows you to customize the Foreground Color (the dots) and the Background Color to match your brand identity. (Tip: Always keep the foreground darker than the background for better scanning).

Do These Codes Expire?

No. We generate Static QR Codes. This means the data is embedded directly into the image. There is no redirect limit, no expiration date, and no tracking. Once you download it, it works forever.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free for commercial use?

Yes. You can use these QR codes on your product packaging, business cards, flyers, and websites without paying any fees.

Why isn't my QR code working?

Make sure there is enough contrast between the colors (Dark code on Light background is best). Also, ensure you didn't enter too much text, as that makes the code very complex and hard to scan.
