Why Word Count Matters for SEO?

Search engines like Google tend to prefer long-form content. While there is no "magic number," studies show that:

  • Blog Posts: Articles between 1,500 to 2,500 words often rank higher.
  • Product Descriptions: Should be concise, typically around 300-500 words.
  • Meta Descriptions: Must be kept under 160 characters to avoid truncation in search results.

Our tool helps you hit these targets precisely without guessing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool accurate?

Yes. Our tool uses advanced logic to distinguish between words and symbols. For example, "It's" is counted as one word, and "High-quality" is also counted as one word, ensuring professional accuracy.

Does it save my text?

No. For your privacy and security, we do not store any text you type on our servers. Everything is processed instantly in your browser (Client-Side).

What counts as a character?

Every letter, number, symbol, and space counts as a character. For example, "Hi!" has 3 characters. "Hi World" has 8 characters (including the space).
