How Does AI Voice Technology Work?

Text-to-Speech technology uses advanced synthesis algorithms to convert phonemes (the smallest units of sound) into spoken words. Our tool leverages the Web Speech API built into modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, and Edge. This allows us to provide:

  • Natural Intonation: The AI understands punctuation (commas, periods, question marks) to pause and inflect correctly.
  • Multiple Accents: Depending on your device, you can access voices in US English, UK English, Hindi, Spanish, and more.
  • Privacy First: Since the synthesis happens locally on your device, your text is never sent to a cloud server, ensuring 100% data privacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I see different voices on my phone vs laptop?

The available voices depend on your Operating System (Windows, macOS, Android, iOS). For example, Windows provides "Microsoft David", while Android provides "Google Voice". Our tool detects and lists all available options on your specific device.

Is there a character limit?

No! Unlike premium services that charge per word, Xizoa Text to Speech allows you to convert unlimited text completely free of charge.

Does it support Hindi or other languages?

Yes. If your device has Hindi, Spanish, French, or any other language pack installed, our tool will automatically list them in the "Select Voice" dropdown menu.
