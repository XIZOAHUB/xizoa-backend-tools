Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this tool safe for confidential documents?

Yes, absolutely. Since we do not store or upload your files, your confidential data remains 100% private. It is as safe as opening the file on your own computer.

Can I reorder the pages?

Currently, the tool merges files in the order they are selected/uploaded. We recommend renaming your files (e.g., 1.pdf, 2.pdf) before selecting them to ensure the correct order. A drag-and-drop reorder feature is coming soon.

What if my PDF is password protected?

If your PDF file is encrypted with a password, our tool cannot access it. You will need to remove the password using a PDF unlocker tool before you can merge it with other files.

Does it reduce the quality of my PDF?

No. Xizoa preserves the original quality of your text and images. Your merged document will look just as crisp and clear as the originals.
