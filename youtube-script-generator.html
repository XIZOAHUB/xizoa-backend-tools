The "Viral Structure" Explained

Our AI-powered templates use the proven structure used by top creators:

  1. The Hook (0:00 - 0:30): Grabs attention instantly. You must promise a value or tease a mystery.
  2. The Intro (0:30 - 1:00): Establish authority. Who are you and why should they listen?
  3. The Meat (Body): Deliver the content quickly without fluff.
  4. The CTA (Call to Action): Ask for the like/subscribe at the right moment (not too early).
  5. The Outro: Send them to another video (End Screen) to increase session time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to follow the script word-for-word?

No! Use this script as a robust outline. Add your own personality, jokes, and examples to make it authentic to you.

How long should my script be?

People speak at about 130-150 words per minute. A 1,000-word script will result in a roughly 6-8 minute video.

What is a "Hook"?

A Hook is the very first sentence of your video. It must confirm to the viewer that they clicked the right video and tease what's coming next.
