Why Use Hashtags on YouTube?

Hashtags (keywords starting with #) are clickable links that users use to find content. In 2025, they are more important than ever, especially for:

  • YouTube Shorts: Shorts rely heavily on tags like #Shorts, #Trending, and #Viral to hit the "Shorts Shelf".
  • Search Discovery: Users often search for specific hashtags (e.g., #GamingSetup) to find ideas.
  • Algorithm Ranking: Hashtags give YouTube strong signals about what your video is about, improving your SEO score.

Pro Tips for Hashtag Strategy

  • Don't Overdo It: For long-form videos, YouTube recommends using 3-5 hashtags. These first 3 tags appear above your video title.
  • Go Wild on Shorts: For YouTube Shorts, you can use more hashtags (10-15) to maximize reach, but make sure they are relevant.
  • Mix It Up: Use one "Brand" tag (your channel name), two "Broad" tags (e.g., #Gaming), and two "Niche" tags (e.g., #MinecraftBuilds).
