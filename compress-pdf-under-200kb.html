Why Do You Need to Resize PDF to 200KB?

Government servers handle millions of applications. To prevent server crashes, they impose strict size limits. Common portals requiring small PDFs include:

  • UPSC Civil Services (IAS/IPS): Requires scanned documents under specific limits.
  • SSC CGL / CHSL / MTS: Photo and signature uploads have very tight constraints.
  • IBPS & SBI PO/Clerk: Banking exams are notorious for strict document resizing rules.
  • College Admissions (DU, BHU, JNU): Marksheets and Caste Certificates must be compressed.
  • Scholarship Portals: National Scholarship Portal (NSP) requires optimized documents.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Problem: My file is still above 200KB.

Solution: If your original PDF contains high-resolution photos, basic compression might not be enough. Move the slider to 0.2 or 0.1 and try again. Alternatively, crop the image before converting to PDF.

Problem: The text looks blurry.

Solution: You have over-compressed the file. Move the slider to the right (towards 0.7 or 0.8) and compress again. You need to find the "Sweet Spot" between size and clarity.
