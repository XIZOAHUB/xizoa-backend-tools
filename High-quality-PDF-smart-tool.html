Why Use Xizoa PDF Tools?

Managing digital documents can be frustrating. You have a PDF but need the text for a report. Or you have 10 photos of notes that need to be sent as a single file. Most online tools ask you to upload your personal files to their servers, which is a huge privacy risk. Others limit you to 2 files a day unless you pay.

Xizoa is different. We built these tools using "Client-Side" technology. This means the code runs inside your browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge). Your files never leave your computer. It’s safer, faster, and completely free.

Tool Features & How They Work

1. Extract Text from PDF

This tool is a lifesaver for students and researchers. Instead of re-typing entire paragraphs from a PDF, upload it here. Our engine scans the document and pulls out the raw text. You can then copy-paste it into Word, Docs, or Notepad. Note: This works best on standard PDFs, not scanned images.

2. Image to PDF Converter

Great for organizing receipts, ID cards, or class notes. Select multiple JPG or PNG images from your gallery. We stitch them together into a clean, professional PDF document that is easy to share via WhatsApp or Email.

3. PDF to Image (JPG)

Sometimes you need just one page of a PDF to post on social media or insert into a presentation. This tool converts PDF pages into high-resolution images. No screenshots needed—you get the full quality.

4. PDF Compression

Email attachments often have size limits (like 25MB). Our compression tool removes unnecessary metadata and optimizes the internal structure of the PDF to reduce its file size, making it easier to send.
