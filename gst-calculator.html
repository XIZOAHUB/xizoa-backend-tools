Best Online GST Calculator for India

The Goods and Services Tax (GST) has transformed India's tax system. Whether you are a business owner generating invoices or a consumer verifying a bill, calculating GST accurately is essential. Xizoa Hub's GST Calculator simplifies this process with two modes: adding tax to a base price (Exclusive) and removing tax from a final price (Inclusive).

Exclusive vs Inclusive GST

  • GST Exclusive (Add GST): Use this when you have the product price and need to add tax on top of it.
    Formula: Price + (Price × Rate / 100)
  • GST Inclusive (Remove GST): Use this when you have the final MRP and want to know the actual product cost and tax amount.
    Formula: Total Amount × 100 / (100 + Rate)

Current GST Tax Slabs

India follows a multi-tier tax structure for different goods and services:

  1. 5%: Essential items like food grains, medicines, transport.
  2. 12%: Processed foods, mobiles, construction work.
  3. 18%: Most consumer goods, electronics, IT services, restaurants (standard rate).
  4. 28%: Luxury items, cars, cement, tobacco.

Understanding CGST, SGST & IGST

When you see the tax breakdown on our tool, you will notice CGST and SGST:

  • CGST (Central GST): Goes to the Central Government.
  • SGST (State GST): Goes to the State Government.
  • IGST (Integrated GST): Applicable for inter-state transactions (one state to another).

For a standard intra-state transaction, the total GST is split equally (e.g., 18% becomes 9% CGST + 9% SGST).
