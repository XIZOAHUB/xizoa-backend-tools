Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the XIZOA HUB website and all associated tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy, especially because most of our tools operate completely client-side.

1. Information We DO NOT Collect

Your privacy is our number one priority. We operate under a strict policy of minimizing data collection.

  • No Personal Data: We do not require registration, login, or personal information (like name, email, or physical address) to use any of our tools.
  • No File Upload Retention: When you upload a file (e.g., for PDF Merger or Image Resizer), the processing happens in your browser. The file is neither sent to nor stored on our servers.
  • No Input Logging: We do not log the text, URLs, or passwords you input into our tools (e.g., Password Generator or Link Scanner).

2. Client-Side Processing (Your Security Guarantee)

The vast majority of XIZOA HUB's tools function entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • Local Processing: Your data (files, text, inputs) never leaves your device for processing.
  • Instant Results: Because there is no server upload/download cycle, results are delivered instantly and securely.

3. Third-Party Services (Analytics & Hosting)

We use minimal third-party services necessary for maintaining and securing the website:

  • **Website Hosting:** Our hosting provider (or CDN like Cloudflare) may collect standard, non-personal traffic logs (e.g., IP address, browser type) for security and performance purposes.
  • **Analytics:** We may use simple, privacy-focused analytics (if any) to understand which tools are popular, but this data is anonymized and generalized.

4. External Links

Our website may contain links to other sites. We have no control over, and are not responsible for, the content or privacy practices of these third-party sites.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
