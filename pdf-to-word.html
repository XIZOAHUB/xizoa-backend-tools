Convert PDF to Word Online for Free

Editing a PDF file can be a nightmare. PDFs were designed to be "read-only," which makes extracting text from them difficult. Xizoa's PDF to Word Converter solves this problem by extracting all the text from your document and saving it as an editable Microsoft Word (.doc) file.

Whether you need to edit a resume, copy text from a research paper, or modify a legal contract, our tool handles the heavy lifting instantly. Best of all, it works entirely in your browser, ensuring your private data stays private.

How It Works

  1. Upload: Select your PDF file from your computer or mobile.
  2. Extraction: Our smart engine reads the PDF page by page and extracts all text content.
  3. Download: The extracted text is automatically formatted into a Word document which you can download and edit immediately.

Key Features

1. 100% Secure (Client-Side)

Unlike other tools that upload your sensitive documents to a cloud server, Xizoa processes everything locally on your device. Your file never leaves your computer, making it the safest way to convert documents.

2. Unlimited Conversions

We don't believe in limits. Convert as many files as you need, whenever you need. No hidden fees or subscriptions.

3. Works Everywhere

Our tool is compatible with Windows, Mac, Linux, Android, and iOS. You can convert files on the go using your smartphone.

Important Note About Images & Formatting

Please note that this tool is designed for Text Extraction. If your PDF contains scanned images of text (where you cannot select the text with your mouse), this tool may not be able to read it without OCR (Optical Character Recognition).

Also, complex layouts like multi-column magazines might be converted into a single stream of text. This tool is best suited for documents like essays, contracts, letters, and reports where text is the priority.
