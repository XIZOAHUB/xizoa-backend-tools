Available Case Modes

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all letters to capitals. Useful for headers or emphasizing warnings. (e.g., "HELLO WORLD").
  • lowercase: Converts all letters to small letters. (e.g., "hello world").
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes the first letter of each sentence and leaves the rest lower. Standard for English writing. (e.g., "Hello world. How are you?").
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of every word. Ideal for blog titles and names. (e.g., "Hello World").
  • aLtErNaTiNg cAsE: A fun style often used on social media or memes. (e.g., "hElLo wOrLd").

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this tool fix grammar?

No, this is strictly a case converter. "Sentence Case" will capitalize the start of sentences based on periods (.), but it won't fix spelling errors.

Is my text saved?

No. Your privacy is our priority. All text processing happens instantly in your browser. We do not store or read your content.
