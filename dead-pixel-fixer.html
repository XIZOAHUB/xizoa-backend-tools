What is a Dead vs. Stuck Pixel?

A **Dead Pixel** is a black dot that doesn't light up. It usually cannot be fixed. A **Stuck Pixel** is a dot stuck on one color (Red, Green, or Blue). These can often be fixed by rapidly changing colors to "massage" the pixel back to life.
