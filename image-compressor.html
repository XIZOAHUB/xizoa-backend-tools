Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Does compressing reduce image quality?

Technically, yes, as we use "lossy" compression to remove unused data. However, our algorithm is tuned so that the visual difference is almost unnoticeable to the human eye, even at 70-80% compression.

What is the best compression level?

For websites, we recommend a quality level between 60% to 80%. This provides the best balance between small file size and good visual clarity.

Can I compress images on my phone?

Yes! Xizoa Image Compressor works perfectly on Android and iPhone browsers (Chrome, Safari, etc.).
