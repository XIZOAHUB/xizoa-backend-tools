About This Tool

This Free AI Text Summarizer uses advanced browser-based logic to condense articles, essays, and reports into easy-to-read summaries. It works instantly without uploading your data to any server.

Key Features

  • Mind Maps: Visual diagrams are generated automatically to show topic connections.
  • Text-to-Speech: Listen to your summary with one click.
  • Privacy: 100% Client-side processing. Safe and Secure.

How it Works

We use a custom TextRank algorithm to identify the most important sentences based on keyword frequency and context. The top-ranked sentences are combined to form the summary.
