What is Mouse Polling Rate?

Polling Rate (measured in Hz) defines how often your mouse reports its position to the computer. A higher polling rate (e.g., **1000Hz**) means less lag and smoother movement, which is crucial for competitive gaming (FPS games like CS:GO, Valorant).
