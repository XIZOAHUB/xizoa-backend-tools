Free YouTube Tag Generator (SEO Tool)

Tags are descriptive keywords you can add to your video to help viewers find your content. YouTube's algorithm uses tags to understand the topic and category of your video. Xizoa Hub's Rapid Tag Generator creates a list of highly relevant, high-traffic tags based on your main keyword, helping you rank higher in search results.

Why Use a Tag Generator?

Manually thinking of 20 different tags can be exhausting. Our tool does the hard work for you:

  • Relevance: It finds keywords that people are actually searching for.
  • Speed: Generate a full list of 500 characters worth of tags in 1 second.
  • Format: The output is comma-separated, meaning you can copy and paste it directly into the YouTube Studio "Tags" box without any editing.

How to Tag Your Videos Correctly

  1. Main Keyword First: Your first tag should be your exact target keyword (e.g., "Chocolate Cake").
  2. Variations: Use alternative phrases (e.g., "How to make chocolate cake", "Easy cake recipe").
  3. Broad Categories: End with 1-2 broad tags (e.g., "Cooking", "Dessert").
  4. Don't Spam: Never use misleading tags. If your video is about cooking, don't add "Justin Bieber" just to get views.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are tags still important in 2025?

While YouTube says tags play a minimal role compared to Title and Thumbnail, they are still valuable for misspellings and helping the algorithm categorize new content.

How many tags can I use?

YouTube allows up to 500 characters in the tag section. It is recommended to use as much space as possible with relevant keywords.
