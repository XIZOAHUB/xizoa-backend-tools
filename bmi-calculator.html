Free BMI Calculator Online

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple screening method used by health professionals to determine if a person is underweight, healthy weight, overweight, or obese. Xizoa Hub's BMI Calculator is designed to be easy to use and provides instant results along with your ideal weight range.

Understanding BMI Categories

The World Health Organization (WHO) defines the following categories for adults:

BMI Range Category Health Risk
Below 18.5 Underweight Malnutrition Risk
18.5 - 24.9 Normal Weight Low Risk
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight Enhanced Risk
30.0 and Above Obese High Risk

Limitations of BMI

While BMI is a useful indicator, it is not perfect. It does not calculate body fat directly. For example:

  • Athletes: May have a high BMI due to muscle mass, not fat.
  • Elderly: May have a normal BMI but low muscle mass.

Tips for a Healthy Weight

Maintaining a healthy BMI significantly reduces the risk of heart disease, diabetes, and joint pain. Focus on a balanced diet rich in proteins and fiber, drink plenty of water, and aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise daily.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is BMI calculated differently for men and women?

The standard BMI formula is the same for adult men and women. However, women naturally have more body fat than men, so interpretation might vary slightly in clinical settings.

How to calculate BMI manually?

The formula is: Weight (kg) / [Height (m)]². For example, if you weigh 70kg and are 1.75m tall: 70 / (1.75 * 1.75) = 22.86.
