YouTube Thumbnail Previewer & CTR Tester

Your video might have the best content in the world, but if nobody clicks on it, the algorithm will bury it. CTR (Click-Through Rate) is one of the most critical metrics for YouTube growth. The decision to click happens in milliseconds, and it depends entirely on your Thumbnail and Title.

Xizoa Hub's Thumbnail Previewer allows you to see exactly how your video will look on the YouTube homepage, mobile app, and sidebar before you publish it. This helps you avoid common mistakes like text being cut off or colors blending into the background.

Why Previewing on Mobile is Critical

Over 70% of YouTube watch time comes from mobile devices. A thumbnail that looks great on a large desktop monitor might look cluttered and unreadable on a small phone screen. Our tool simulates the mobile view so you can ensure your text is big enough and your face is visible.

Checklist for High CTR Thumbnails

  • Less is More: Keep text under 4-5 words. Use bold, contrasting colors.
  • The "BOGY" Rule: Use Black, Orange, Green, and Yellow backgrounds to stand out against YouTube's white/dark theme.
  • Emotion: Faces with strong emotions (surprise, happiness, anger) tend to get more clicks.
  • Title Synergy: Don't just repeat the title in the thumbnail. Use the thumbnail to tease the visual, and the title to add context.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the perfect YouTube thumbnail size?

The standard size is 1280 x 720 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio). The minimum width is 640 pixels, and the file size should be under 2MB.

Does changing a thumbnail affect views?

Yes, significantly. If an old video isn't performing well, changing the thumbnail to something more catchy can "revive" the video and boost its CTR, leading to more impressions from YouTube.
