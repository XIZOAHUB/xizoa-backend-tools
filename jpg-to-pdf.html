How to Convert Images to PDF for Free

Turning photos into a PDF file is one of the most common tasks for students and professionals. Whether you have clicked pictures of your handwritten notes, scanned copies of your ID cards (Aadhaar/PAN), or screenshots of a payment receipt, keeping them as separate image files is messy.

Xizoa's JPG to PDF Converter solves this instantly. You can select multiple images at once, and our tool stitches them together into a single, clean PDF document. It works with JPG, JPEG, and PNG formats perfectly.

Who Needs This Tool?

1. Students & Exam Aspirants

Most online exam forms and college assignments require you to upload a single PDF file containing all your answer sheets. Instead of using heavy scanner apps that add watermarks, simply take photos and use our tool to merge them into one PDF.

2. Office Professionals

Sending 10 different image attachments in an email looks unprofessional. Convert your invoices, reports, or meeting notes into one professional PDF document before hitting send.

3. Government Form Applicants

Applying for jobs or schemes often requires uploading documents like marks sheets and caste certificates. These are usually images on your phone. Convert them to PDF to ensure they are accepted by the portal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I convert PNG files?

Yes, this tool supports both JPG and PNG formats. You can even mix them in a single PDF.

Does it reduce quality?

We maintain high quality so your text remains readable. However, we do optimize the file size slightly so it's easy to share via email or WhatsApp.

How do I reorder images?

Currently, images are added in the order you select them. For best results, rename your files (e.g., 1.jpg, 2.jpg) before selecting, or select them one by one in order.
