How to Write a Job-Winning Resume?

Follow these expert tips to maximize your chances:

  • Keep it Concise: Recruiters spend about 6 seconds scanning a resume. Keep your summary short and impactful.
  • Use Action Verbs: Start bullet points with words like "Led," "Developed," "Increased," or "Managed."
  • Quantify Achievements: Instead of saying "Improved sales," say "Increased sales by 20% in Q3."
  • Tailor to the Job: Use keywords from the job description in your "Skills" section.
